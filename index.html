EXCLUSIVE - Lipton Tea & Infusions changes its name once more

1 minute read 1 April, 2023 04:20 UTC

The world’s largest Tea company is changing its name once more.

The privately owned company formerly known as ekaterra, recently renamed to Lipton Tea & Infusions, is now changing its name once more to "Lippy", according to a source close to the matter. Speaking on the condition of anonymity, the source said that the name reflects a spirit of “Love, Fun, Cheekiness, an Entrepreneurial Spirit, and not taking life too seriously”.

The source also hinted that further details will be announced after midday today.
